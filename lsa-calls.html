Google LSA Automation Workflow

Automatic polling pipeline runs every 30 minutes

1. Fetch LSA Leads & Audio (API) Polls the Google LSA API automatically every 30 minutes to retrieve new lead metadata and raw call audio recordings.
2. Audio-to-Text Transcription Converts the call audio recording into a clean, speaker-separated text transcription using advanced LLM speech models.
3. LLM Lead Context Analysis Feeds the lead info and transcription to the LLM to identify job type, customer location, booking status, and refund eligibility.
4. Dashboard Synchronization Populates all parsed metrics into this audit dashboard, highlighting invalid leads so you can submit cost-saving disputes instantly.

Google SEM Automation Workflow

Webhook triggers automatically on new GHL call

1. Receive GHL Webhook (Real-Time) GoHighLevel (GHL) triggers a webhook immediately after an SEM call ends, pushing the customer metadata and raw transcript payload.
2. AI Transcription & Lead Analysis Sends the transcript details directly to the LLM to verify search campaign attribution, identify buyer intent, and evaluate lead status.
3. Dashboard Synchronization Streams structured SEM conversion results directly to the dashboard, displaying qualified bookings alongside spam/solicitation alerts.
